how to make it work

you're going on a date! amazing.
soo.. what'll you talk about?
  • your ex and all the details as to why it didn't work out
  • how many of your friends are getting married this year - and on a related note, how you'll need to find someone to watch your cat for the out of town weddings
  • no need to plan this - you can also, like, listen instead of thinking about what you're going to say next while your date is talking
phew- the date went great (at least as far as you can tell)!
soo.. what's text etiquette from here?
  • wait a day, then say "heyy" (two "y"s is important) - followed by "how's it going?" - then have a normal conversation and see how it goes
  • write a message before you even get home from the date that's at least two iPhone screens long - highlight why you're a perfect match and lay out some days/times they can meet your parents
  • never - you don't text initiate, you only respond to texts! OH and before you do, you will ask at least 3 friends to curate and then re-read it compulsively to ensure it's right - & repeat with each new reply needed
so you're in love
how do you show it?
  • by clinging on for dear life- let the other person know things like: how obsessed with them you are, how many of your past relationships have failed before you found them, and make big vacation plans together for a far off day bc there's no way it won't fail
  • by listening, doing small things to make them feel appreciated, and by being there for them even when shitty stuff happens, even it it's inconvenient and uncomfortable for you
  • never let it show - and in fact, don't even admit it to yourself - that's how you get hurt!
